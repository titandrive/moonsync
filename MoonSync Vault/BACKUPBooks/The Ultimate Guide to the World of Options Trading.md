# The Ultimate Guide to the World of Options Trading
**Author:** Options Profits Daily

## Highlights

> [!quote]
> A stock with a beta of 1.0 has mirrored the market's performance. Stocks with betas of less than 1 (but greater than 0) have been less volatile than the broad market, and stocks with betas higher than 1 have been more volatile.

> [!quote]
> Another reason to buy puts is if for some reason you are restricted from selling stock you own for a set period of time.

> [!quote]
> Imagine this: Your grandma leaves you 10,000 shares of AT&T (Ticker: T) in her will, but the stock is held up in probate.

> [!quote]
> On August 7, 2008, Best Buy was trading at $40.43. If you were willing to buy 100 shares at $35, you could write a December put contract with a $35 strike price and receive a $730 premium.
